# GPG Gathering Place Global

GPG Gathering Place Global is a high-trust, faith-centered digital ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between social networking, spiritual growth, and economic self-reliance. Built specifically for the Young Single Adult (YSA) community, BYU-Pathway Worldwide students, and friends of the Church, it serves as a "Digital Sanctuary" and a "Success Engine."

## 1. Core Purpose: Why GPG Exists

In a world of noisy and often unsafe social media, GPG provides a Safe Harbor. It is designed to:

- **Gather the Community:** Move the "Gathering Place" experience from a once-a-week physical event to a 24/7 global digital connection.
- **Foster Self-Reliance:** Connect education (BYU-Pathway) directly to the economy (Marketplace).
- **Protect the Environment:** Use AI-driven "Gospel Standards" moderation to eliminate bullying and harassment.

## 2. The Three Pillars of the App

### Pillar 1: The Spiritual & Social Network

- **Mission Peer Discovery:** A specialized search engine to find and reconnect with companions and members from specific missions worldwide.
- **Wholesome Feed:** A "Facebook-style" video and photo feed where users share testimonies, service projects, and uplifting content.
- **Gender-Specific Fellowship:** Private groups for men and women to discuss leadership, parenting, and faith-specific growth.

### Pillar 2: The Academic Hub (BYU-Pathway)

- **Status Tracking:** Profiles clearly show if a user is in PathwayConnect, a Degree program, or is an Alumni.
- **Local Study Groups:** Automatic grouping based on a user's LGA (Local Government Area) and degree major (e.g., Software Development or Applied Health).
- **Peer Mentorship:** Alumni are incentivized to mentor current students, creating a cycle of success.

### Pillar 3: The Talent Marketplace (The Economy)

- **Verified Professional Directory:** A "Yellow Pages" for the community. Users can find trusted electricians, plumbers, tutors, or coders.
- **Merit-Based Access:** While there is a small listing fee ($2), the Admin can grant "Merit Access" to high-achieving students or those in need to help them start their businesses.
- **High-Trust Hiring:** Features like Genotype/Blood Group (optional/private) and Mission History build a level of trust that generic platforms like LinkedIn cannot match.

## 3. The "Power House" Control Room (For You)

As the owner, you manage the ecosystem through an advanced Admin Dashboard:

- **AI Guardrails:** Llama 4 AI monitors chats for harassment, sending "Red Flags" to you only when a violation occurs to protect user privacy.
- **Global Heatmaps:** Visualize where the "Gathering" is growing in real-time, from Lagos to Salt Lake City.
- **Sovereign Management:** Ability to approve ads, suspend bad actors, and manually promote talented individuals to the marketplace.

## 4. Brand Identity & Experience

- **Colors:** Deep Harbor Navy (Security), Celestial Gold (Divinity), and Pathway Amber (Energy).
- **The Vibe:** Professional, sacred, and modern.
- **Tech Stack:** Built with Flutter for speed, Node.js for reliability, and Llama 4 AI for safety.
